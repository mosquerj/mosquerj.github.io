Classic Meatballs

These classic Italian meatballs are tender, flavorful, and perfect for any occasion. Serve them with pasta, in a sub, or as an appetizer!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Form into meatballs.
  3. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes.

Tips

For extra flavor, try adding a pinch of garlic powder or grated Parmesan cheese to the mixture. Serve with your favorite marinara sauce!
